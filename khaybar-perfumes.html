Notre Maison

Born from the
ancient trade routes.

Khaybar is named after the legendary oasis city of Arabia — a crossroads of cultures, caravans, and rare treasures. Our perfumes carry that same spirit: bold, storied, and unforgettable.

Every bottle is the result of meticulous sourcing from the world's finest regions — Indian oud, Bulgarian rose, Omani frankincense, and Moroccan amber — blended by master perfumers with decades of craft.

The art of crafting scent

01

Ingredient Sourcing

Our master blenders travel to India, Cambodia, Oman, and Morocco to hand-select each raw material at peak harvest.

02

Distillation

Traditional steam distillation and cold-press extraction preserve every nuance of the raw ingredients, unchanged.

03

Blending

Our perfumers hand-blend each accord in small batches, allowing each layer to marry before adding the next.

04

Bottling & Aging

Each blend rests for a minimum of 6 weeks before bottling — allowing the fragrance to fully mature and harmonize.
